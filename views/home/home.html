About Us

MentorRock is a web application focusing on helping Students in UofT build long-term mentor-mentee relationship.

Whether you are currently confused about your career planning, or need some help for writing your first resume, or even just need some advice as a swimming beginner, here is the right place for you. There are mentors from all department, of all fields. You could chat with them online, or schedule an off-line meeting if you want to.

If you are proficient in some fields, join us as mentor today!
